Desert Rat at Heart

While I've now living in Austin Texas for over ten years, I am still very much a desert rat at heart. I spend most of my life in Southern Arizona, where hot has a whole new definition.

The best smell in the world is the desert right before it rains

It's funny the things you get excited about when you spent most of your life in the desert.

Water
Large bodies of water seem miraculous to me.
Shade
You have never seen anything funnier then a bunch of people parking in the boonies, just so they can get a shady spot
